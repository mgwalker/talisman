Talisman

Version 0.1.a, © Copyright 2002, Jason Pike and Greg Walker

Talisman is a computer game based on the 1980's fantasy role-playing game by Games Workshop. Players roll dice and move around the game board. Actions must be taken according to which board tiles the player lands on, including fighting enemies or other players already on the same tile.
